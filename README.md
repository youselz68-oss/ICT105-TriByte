## Initial Problem Area
International students struggle to find useful information about dorms, cafeterias, and campus life when they first arrive at university.

## Target Users
International students in their first semester at university.

## Initial IT Venture Direction
A mobile app where students can read and write reviews about dorms and cafeterias, and find helpful guides for settling into university life.
